Things to Know about Chameleons

Chameleons change color based on their mood and don't fully develop their color until they reach adulthood (About 5 Months). Since Chameleons live exclusively in trees, their tanks should be big (At least 3 by 3 feet) and have lots of foliage. In addition, make sure their tank is warm because Chameleons thrive in warmer environments. They eat insects, so ensure their diet includes crickets.
