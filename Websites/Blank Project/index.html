About Grime Reaper

Grime Reaper is a car washing business based in Seattle, Washington. We have many locations throughout the PNW.

We are a family-owned business that has been operating for over 20 years. Our mission is to provide the best car washing experience in the Pacific Northwest.

We use eco-friendly products and methods to ensure that our car washes are safe for the environment. Our team is dedicated to providing excellent customer service and making sure your car looks its best.

We offer a variety of car wash packages to suit your needs, from basic washes to full detailing services. We also have a loyalty program that rewards our customers for their continued support.
