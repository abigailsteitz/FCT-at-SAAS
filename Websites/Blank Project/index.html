Basketball

Rules and Objectives

Basketball is a team sport where two teams of five players each compete to score points by shooting a ball through the opposing teams hoop, which is mounted 10 feet high. The main objective is to score more points than the opponent by the end of four quarters, each typically lasting 12 minutes. Players can move the ball by dribbling or passing, but must follow rules such as not traveling (moving without dribbling) or committing fouls (illegal physical contact). The game emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and skill, with points awarded for field goals (2 or 3 points) and free throws (1 point each). .
