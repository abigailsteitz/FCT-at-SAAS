About clothing brands

Many clothing brands can be very harmful to the enviornment, so I am here to tell you the affordable and enviornmentally friendly brands to shop from!

Learn More

Affordable and enviornmentally friendly:

  • Pact
  • ThreadUP
  • For Days
  • Depop

Prices

All of these stores are very affordable! Pacts clothing is 10$-15$, ThreadUP is $5-$40. For days is $20-$60.Depop is a re-selling company so prices vary, bit it is relatively cheap.

About the brands

  1. Pact uses Organic cotton and fair trade certified factories.
  2. For days is a Closed-loop recycling program and they send back old clothes for store credit
  3. ThredUP Reuses and reduces waste model.
  4. Depop is a re-selling company that allows you to sell your old clothes.

There is a way to save money and help the enviornment!

These brands are not the only ones though, you can easily find many affordable brands that also prioritize being enviornmentally friendly!
